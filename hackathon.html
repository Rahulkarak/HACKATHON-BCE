Techspardha: A Platform for Aspiring Tech enthusitic


Techspardha was created as a platform to help engineering students in industrial level training



About Techspardha Hackathon:


Techspardha Hackathon is a yearly contest dedicated to the programmers on the platform. A 48-hour Develepment contest which takes place on every year in month of March.



Contest Details:


Duration: 48 hours
Start Date: Wednesday, 02nd March, 2023 at 20:00 HRS (IST)
End Date: Wednesday, 04st March, 2023 at 23:00 HRS (IST)
Eligibility Criteria: Anyone with a knack for Programming .
Our contests are open to all the engineering student across the globe.

Venue : Bhagalpur College of Engineering ,Bhagalpur ,Bihar .



What's in it for you?


The idea behind these Develepment contests is that we want you to learn while competing. Also we believe that it is alright to refer to tutorials, books and other materials, learn a concept and then apply the same to solve a problem during a contest. But it is not alright to copy other people's solutions or seek other people's help to solve a problem. All the participants are expected to abide to Techspardha Code Of Conduct



.

Rules and Regulations:


1. Team Size: maximum 3 members are allowed for a team.
2. Every participant must carry their own institute's original ID card.
3. Theme: The event will have a specific theme or problem statement that will be provided at the start of the event.
4. Participation Fee - 50 per team member.
5. Time Limit: The event will typically be a 40 hours long competition. The exact duration will be specified in the event announcement.
6. Equipment: Participants are expected to bring their own laptops and any other equipment they may need for the competition.
7. Judging Criteria: The solutions developed will be evaluated based on the following criteria:
a. Innovation and Creativity
b. User Experience and Design
c. Technical Complexity
d. Impact and Feasibility
8. Prizes: Prizes will be awarded to the top two teams, and other incentives will be announced at the start of the event.
9. Code of Conduct: Participants are expected to follow a code of conduct that promotes respect, inclusion, and teamwork.

Note:
● Judge's decision will be the final.
● The event rules may be subject to change, and any changes will be communicated to the participants in advance.
● Teams can be disqualified from the competition at the organizers’ discretion. Reasons might include, but are not limited to, breaking the Competition Rules or other unsporting behavior.
