What's in it for you?


The idea behind these exibition contests is that we want you to learn while competing. Also we believe that it is alright to refer to tutorials, books and other materials, learn a concept and then apply the same to solve a problem during a contest. But it is not alright to copy other people's solutions or seek other people's help to solve a problem. All the participants are expected to abide to Techspardha Code Of Conduct



<--GUIDELINES-->


--> A maximum of 4 members per team are allowed.


--> Participation is open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students


--> Students from different colleges can also be a part of the same team.


--> Winning teams of the last 3 years cannot participate with the same projects as they had last time. However, if they have a new idea no such restriction holds.

--> All entrants are regarded as having a full understanding of the regulations for the competition and being willing to comply with every rule.

--> The decision of the judges and the event managers will be final and binding.

--> Apart from the best model a special jury award for the best presentation.
